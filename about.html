About Mako Parents

A parent-powered hub for Orange Beach families

Who We Are

Mako Parents is an independent, parent-run site created by families in Orange Beach. We’re not affiliated with the school district—we’re simply parents who believe in sharing clear, timely, and accessible information so every family feels informed, not overwhelmed.

Why We Exist

Every parent deserves a seat at the table. Too often, important updates are buried in agendas, emails, or Facebook threads. We built Mako Parents to bring everything together in one place—meeting minutes, events, resources, and special education guidance—so you can focus on supporting your kids instead of chasing down information.

How You Can Help

  • Share: Send us minutes, links, or reminders using the Submit form.
  • Spread the word: Tell other parents about the site.
  • Stay involved: Attend board meetings, ask questions, and be part of the conversation.

Contact Us

Have questions, ideas, or corrections? Reach out through our Submit form.
